Our Simple
Terms & Conditions:

By clicking "Sign Up Now", you agree to subscribe to a weekly delivery of handpicked organic fruits and vegetables directly to your home. Your payment method will be verified upon signing up, and your account will be billed for your initial delivery. Purchasing a Gift Card will also be charged instantly, and the card will be sent to the provided email address.

SNAP/EBT Cards will be securely stored, but a payment link will be emailed to you the day before your scheduled delivery. You must log in and enter your PIN to complete the payment. If payment isn't completed by 4am on the delivery day, the order will be cancelled.

For recurring deliveries, your card will be charged automatically each time a delivery is prepared. Billing usually occurs one day before delivery. There's no delivery fee, and you can cancel your subscription anytime. (Please note our 24-hour notice policy below)

You can modify your order as often as needed, but a produce box (or gift basket) must always be included to receive a delivery (i.e., you cannot receive only a bottle of juice and some snacks).

You'll be automatically enrolled to receive our weekly newsletter and other service-related emails from Green Grocers, but you can opt out at any time. We NEVER share or sell your personal information with third parties.

Any changes or cancellations must be made at least 24 HOURS IN ADVANCE. We strive to accommodate last-minute requests, but it's not always possible due to our order commitments with farmers and suppliers. Prices may occasionally increase (typically every few years), but all charges will be clearly detailed on your receipt. The easiest way to manage your account and view the current status is through your member page, but we're also available to assist via phone or email. Leaving a voicemail 24 hours in advance also counts as valid notice.

Please review and confirm your order details, including items, frequencies, and billing information, before clicking Sign Up Now.

Thank you for choosing us.
Your support means a lot!

~ The Green Grocers Team
